About me:

I have been a computer programmer, professionally, for over 10 years. I was lucky to cut my teeth as a Junior Server-side Developer at Jaywing, at the time the largest Digital Agency outside of London, before moving on to Plusnet as a Senior Client-Side developer, then Castus as a Senior Server-Side developer,before my current role as Web Developer at the University of Derby.


I am a passionate engineer, interested in pushing the envelope of web-technology, and seeing how much can be done with as little as possible (hence some of the ethos behind this site), and in making the sophisticated seem simple. I believe that the web should be accessible, and that technology should not come at the expense of people's ability to access it. I believe in a free and open web.
